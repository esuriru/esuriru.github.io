Their Last Ride

Their Last Ride is a game that was created in 2 weeks during my first semester of my diploma in NYP (Nanyang Polytechnic).

It was created in collaboration with my groupmates Ray, Tania, Paul and Jude during our Studio Project which we were randomly allocated as groupmates together.

Story

There is a fine line between life and death, and in this world, it's a train line. You are the Ticketmaster, and it is up to you to determine which passengers belong to the world of the living and the world of the dead.

Takeaways

Team

It was my first time working in a team, especially with GitHub, I had never collaborated using it, so it was a huge pain to deal with merge conflicts.

My groupmates had also no experience with GitHub so I had to teach them along the way. I thought that the overhead of teaching my groupmates was way easier than not havintg the convenience of GitHub.

More/resultant problems on GitHub include:

  • Doing all our tasks on main branch
  • Undescriptive commit messages
  • Working with two versions of Visual Studio

Though, that last one is not really GitHub's fault.

A naming convention was also not thoroughly enforced, hence resulting in inconsistent naming of variables, functions and the like.
